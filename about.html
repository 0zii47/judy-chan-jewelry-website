About Judy

A designer raised around gold, now working in California light.

Born in Singapore and based in Beverly Hills, Judy Chan creates fine jewelry that lets each piece tell a story.

Her story

From Serangoon gold counters to a Wilshire atelier.

Judy grew up around her father Chen Long's jewelry shop on Serangoon Road, where gold, gemstones, and family milestones shared the same counter. She completed a master's degree in applied economics at the National University of Singapore in 2013, but the workbench remained her strongest pull.

After moving to Los Angeles in 2016, she founded Chan Fine Jewelry in Beverly Hills. Today a small design studio and trusted LA Jewelry District makers shape engagement jewels, sculptural colored-stone pieces, and bespoke heirlooms.

At Judy's desk

Every commission begins with a clear point of view.

Judy works from conversation, proportion, and the character of a stone before a jewel moves toward the bench.

Sketches hold the first rhythm of a piece: how a pendant rests at the collarbone, where a bracelet catches light, and which detail should stay quiet enough to feel timeless.
